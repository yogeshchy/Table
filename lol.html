Bhadragol is a Nepali sitcom that airs every Friday at 8:50 pm NST on Nepal Television. It is one of the most viewed television programs in Nepal. The show was formerly produced by Subash Karki but now Media Hub Pvt. Ltd. produces the show.[1] Kumar Katel and Arjun Ghimire are the writers and the directors of the show. The story is based on village life.[2] The show stars Arjun Ghimire, Kumar Katel, Deepak Acharya, Rakshya Shrestha, Sagar Lamsal, Kamalmani Nepal, Madhu Sudan Pathak, Ashok Dhital, Hari Niraula and more.The show revolves round the daily happenings of a village in Nepal. Jigri is a young crook who, along with his fellows Baley and Cockroach, commits petty crimes. Jigri also loves Rakshya and tries to marry her in most of the episodes but fails everytime because of Kaku.Paadey Sharma, commonly known as Paadey, is the most cunning man in the entire village. He is expert in fooling and cheating the villagers with his funny ideas. His nephew, Jayente, often supports him in his misdeeds.
